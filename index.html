Our Story

We are small show hobby breeders, located in Southern California in the Irvine/Lake Forest area.

  • 2010

    Beginnings

    oagijaewfiojaewfoijIn early 2010, we brought home our first chinchilla. We quickly fell in love with their playful and adorable personality.

  • 2012

    Breeding

    We attended our first ECBC educational field day and decided to start breeding high quality chinchillas by adding a few high quality breeders from DarkStar and Shoots Ranch.

  • 2014

    ECBC and Shows

    We became proud members of Empress Chinchilla Breeders Cooperative (ECBC). We have been actively showing chinchillas since 2014. We have done well with a few Grand Show Champion awards and many first place chinchillas.

  • 2016

    Awards and Recognition

    At ECBC 2016 National Show in Ventura, we are very proud of our three black velvets are awarded with Grand Show Champion, Reserved Grand Show Champion and 3rd best of the show and many of our standards grey are awarded with first place or higher. It has been an exciting journey for us and our herd. We look forward to continuing to breed and improve chinchillas quality in conformation, size, and clarity on the show tables.

For Sale



Care Package

Each chinchilla will go home with a care package, including some essential supplies and a care sheet. The package includes 1lb of pellet, some hay cubes, organic apple chew sticks, shredded wheat and dust bath. The care sheet has information on experienced exotic vets in Southern California and basic chinchilla care. This will help your new chinchilla transition its new feed and environment.


Special Requests

Chinchilla price varies upon availability, quality and color. Young/Baby chinchillas for sale will come home with a care package and a card (only available upon request) with DOB and color of parents. Pedigreed breeding stock is available upon request and will come home with three generation pedigree card.



Payment Method

50% deposit (non-refundable) is required to place any chinchillas on hold. Full payment is required prior transportation. We only accept payment in cash or by PayPal.



Delivery

Direct delivery is available with a fee in Greater Los Angeles, Orange County and San Diego areas twice a month. Please set up a date with us! Transportation is also available to the upcoming shows we plan to attend. For more information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
